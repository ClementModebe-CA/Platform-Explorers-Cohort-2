# Use Case 3: Customer Support and Contact Center

## 🎯 Scenario
MAXi needs to efficiently handle increasing customer complaints and service requests.

---

## 🧩 Solution (Dynamics 365 Customer Service)

Use standard entities:

- Cases
- Knowledge Base
- Queues

---

## ⚙️ Implementation

- Create support cases from:
  - Email (Outlook integration)
  - Manual entry
- Assign cases to support agents
- Track lifecycle:
  - Active → Resolved → Closed

---

## 🤖 AI and Automation

- AI-powered case classification and routing
- Suggested knowledge articles for agents
- Chatbots for basic customer inquiries
- Sentiment analysis from customer messages

---

## 🎓 Student Assignment

- Create 3 cases
- Assign cases to support agents
- Resolve at least 1 case
- Create 1 knowledge base article

---

## ✅ Expected Outcome

- Faster case resolution
- Improved customer satisfaction
