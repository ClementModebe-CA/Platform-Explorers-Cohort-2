# 🧪 Lab 1: Lead to Opportunity Process

## 🎯 Objective
Configure and execute the lead qualification process.

---

## 🔧 Steps

- Go to Sales Hub
- Navigate to Leads
- Create a new Lead:
  - Name
  - Email
  - Preferred Bicycle Type (custom field)
  - Budget (custom field)
- Save the Lead

---

## 🔄 Qualify Lead

- Click **Qualify**
- Ensure system creates:
  - Account
  - Contact
  - Opportunity

---

## 🤖 Automation Task

Create a Power Automate flow:

- Trigger: When Lead is created
- Action: Send notification email

---

## ✅ Expected Result

- Lead successfully converted
- Opportunity created
- Notification triggered
