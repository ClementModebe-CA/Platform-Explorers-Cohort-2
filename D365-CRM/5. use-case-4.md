# Use Case 4: Field Service Management

## 🎯 Scenario
MAXi provides on-site repairs and maintenance through field engineers and needs better coordination.

---

## 🧩 Solution (Dynamics 365 Field Service)

Use standard entities:

- Work Orders
- Resources (Field Engineers)
- Scheduling

---

## ⚙️ Implementation

- Create work orders from support cases
- Assign field engineers
- Schedule service visits
- Track job completion

---

## 🤖 AI and Automation

- AI-based scheduling optimization
- Route optimization for field engineers
- Predictive maintenance insights
- Automated notifications to customers

---

## 🎓 Student Assignment

- Create 2 work orders
- Assign to field engineers
- Schedule service visits
- Mark one job as completed

---

## ✅ Expected Outcome

- Efficient field operations
- Reduced response time
- Better resource utilization
