# 🧪 Lab 3: Case Management

## 🎯 Objective
Manage customer support requests.

---

## 🔧 Steps

- Go to Customer Service Hub
- Navigate to Cases
- Create a new Case:
  - Customer
  - Issue Type
  - Priority

---

## 🔄 Case Lifecycle

- Assign case to agent
- Update status:
  - Active → Resolved

---

## 📘 Knowledge Base

- Create 1 Knowledge Article
- Link it to a case

---

## 🤖 Automation Task

- Auto-assign case to queue

---

## ✅ Expected Result

- Case tracked and resolved
- Knowledge article used
