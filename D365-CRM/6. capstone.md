# 🚴 MAXi End-to-End CRM Implementation (Capstone Project)

## 🎯 Objective
Design and implement a complete CRM system for MAXi covering:

- Sales (Lead → Opportunity → Quote)
- Customer Service (Case Management)
- Field Service (Work Orders and Scheduling)

---

## 🧩 Business Requirement

MAXi needs:

- Better visibility into sales pipeline
- Faster response to customer issues
- Efficient coordination of field engineers
- Automation to reduce manual work

---

## ⚙️ Solution Scope

### 1. Sales Module
- Lead capture and qualification
- Opportunity pipeline with Business Process Flow
- Quote generation

---

### 2. Customer Service
- Case management
- Case lifecycle tracking
- Knowledge base integration

---

### 3. Field Service
- Work order creation
- Resource scheduling
- Service tracking

---

### 4. AI and Automation (MANDATORY)

Implement at least 2 of the following:

- Lead scoring or prioritization
- Case auto-assignment
- Email notification flows
- AI suggestions (next best action)

---

## 📊 Deliverables

### 1. Working CRM System
- Fully configured application
- End-to-end customer journey

---

### 2. Demo Presentation (30 minutes)

Explain:
- Business problem
- Solution design
- Key features

---

### 3. Documentation
- Entities used (standard vs custom)
- Automations implemented

---

## 🏆 Evaluation Criteria

- Use of standard CRM entities (25%)
- Business process implementation (25%)
- Automation and AI usage (20%)
- Usability (15%)
- Presentation (15%)
