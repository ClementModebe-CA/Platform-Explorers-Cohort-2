# Use Case 1: Lead and Opportunity Management (Sales)

## 🎯 Scenario
MAXi wants to efficiently track and convert incoming leads from multiple channels into paying customers.

---

## 🧩 Solution (Dynamics 365 Sales)

Use standard entities:

- Leads
- Accounts
- Contacts
- Opportunities

---

## ⚙️ Implementation

- Capture leads from Outlook or Forms
- Qualify leads into:
  - Account
  - Contact
  - Opportunity
- Track opportunity lifecycle:
  - Qualify → Develop → Propose → Close

---

## 🤖 AI and Automation

- AI-based lead scoring to prioritize high-value customers
- Auto-reminders for follow-ups
- Suggested next actions for sales representatives

---

## 🎓 Student Assignment

- Customize Lead form by adding:
  - Bicycle Preference
  - Budget
- Create 5 leads
- Qualify at least 2 leads
- Create 3 opportunities and move them through the pipeline

---

## ✅ Expected Outcome

- Fully functional sales pipeline
- Improved lead conversion visibility
