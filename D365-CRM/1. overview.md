# MAXi CRM Implementation Project

## 📌 Company Overview
MAXi is a Cape Verdean company that sells bicycles and provides after-sales support services.

- Customers: 10,000+
- Sales Team: 3 personnel
- Support Desk: 5 personnel
- Field Engineers: 7 personnel

---

## ⚠️ Current Challenges
Due to increased sales and support demand, MAXi is experiencing:

- Inefficient sales tracking
- Manual customer data handling (Excel and SharePoint)
- Delayed response to customer issues
- Limited visibility into customer lifecycle
- Overloaded support and field teams

---

## 🏗️ Current Infrastructure
- Microsoft 365 (Outlook, Teams) – Communication
- Microsoft 365 (Excel, SharePoint) – Customer data management
- Custom accounting solution

---

## 💡 Recommended Solution
Implementation of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement apps:

- Dynamics 365 Sales
- Dynamics 365 Customer Service
- Dynamics 365 Field Service
- Contact Center capabilities

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Optimize sales workflow
- Improve lead conversion
- Automate customer service processes
- Enable efficient field service operations
- Introduce AI-driven insights and automation

---

## 🤖 Role of AI and Automation
Using Microsoft AI capabilities:

- Lead scoring and prioritization
- Automated case routing
- AI-powered customer insights
- Suggested actions for sales and support teams
